# Drift Guard

## Anti-Drift Objective
Keep TeamFrame focused on lightweight HR structure infrastructure for startups.

## Forbidden Expansion Areas
- payroll and benefits
- enterprise HRIS modules
- analytics dashboards
- workflow orchestration engines
- notification/reminder systems
- integration ecosystems (Zapier/webhooks)
- compliance automation or advisory
- e-signature or approval/versioning engines
- employee scoring/ranking or personality inference
- employee preference/settings systems

## Enforcement
Any proposed feature that intersects forbidden areas must be rejected or reduced to in-scope V1 behavior.
